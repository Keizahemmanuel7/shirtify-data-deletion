DATA DELETION REQUEST – SHIRTIFY CUSTOM APPAREL STORE APP

This page explains how users of the Shirtify Custom Apparel Store App can request deletion of their account and personal data.

──────────────────────────────

YOUR DATA DELETION OPTIONS

Users may request either of the following:

1. ACCOUNT DELETION

You may request deletion of your Shirtify Custom Apparel Store App account and all associated personal data. Once your request is processed, your account and related information will be permanently removed, except where retention is required by law or legitimate business obligations.

2. SPECIFIC DATA DELETION

You may request deletion of certain personal data collected through the app without deleting your entire account. This allows you to continue using the Shirtify Custom Apparel Store App while removing selected information.

──────────────────────────────

HOW TO REQUEST DATA DELETION

To submit a data deletion request:

1. 

Send an email to: keizahemmanuel4@gmail.com

2. 

Use one of the following subject lines:

- "Account Deletion Request – Shirtify Custom Apparel Store App"

- "Data Deletion Request – Shirtify Custom Apparel Store App"

3. 

Include your account email address and details of the information you would like deleted.

──────────────────────────────

DATA THAT CAN BE DELETED

Depending on your request, we may delete:

- User account information

- Name

- Email address

- Phone number (if provided)

- Delivery or shipping information (if provided)

- Saved preferences

- Wishlist items

- Shopping cart information

- Order history (where applicable)

- Custom apparel design submissions (where applicable)

- Uploaded images, logos, artwork, or other media files

- App usage information associated with your account

- Communication records submitted through the app

──────────────────────────────

ACCOUNT DELETION

If you request account deletion, we will delete your account and associated personal data, except where retention is required for legal, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, order fulfillment, or financial record-keeping purposes.

──────────────────────────────

DATA RETENTION

Certain information may be retained for a limited period if required for:

- Legal obligations

- Fraud prevention

- Security investigations

- Dispute resolution

- Order management and fulfillment records

- Financial or transaction record keeping

After the applicable retention period expires, retained data will be securely deleted.

──────────────────────────────

PROCESSING TIME

We aim to process deletion requests within 7–14 business days, subject to legal and operational requirements.
